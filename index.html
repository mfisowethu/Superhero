THE FLASH

The Fastest Man Alive

Barry Allen

Barry Allen, a forensic scientist for the Central City Police Department, gained his superhuman speed in a freak accident when he was struck by lightning in his lab, doused by chemicals from his shelf.

As The Flash, Barry can run at speeds that approach the speed of light, vibrate through solid objects, and even travel through time.

He is a founding member of the Justice League and protector of Central City.

First Appearance Showcase #4 (1956)
Created By Robert Kanigher & Carmine Infantino

Speed Force Abilities

Superhuman Speed
